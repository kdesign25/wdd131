About Us

Lehman's Big Open Road, located in Dover, OH is a motorcycle shop poised to become the premier destination for motorcycle enthusiasts in the region. The business is built around the passion for motorcycles, offering a wide range of motorcycles, accessories, and maintenance services tailored to meet the needs of both seasoned riders and those new to the biking world. With a strong commitment to customer satisfaction and a deep understanding of the motorcycle lifestyle, our goal is to build a community around our brand, creating a hub for riders to not only purchase and service their bikes but to also engage with fellow enthusiasts. Our location in Dover is strategic, allowing us to tap into a vibrant local market of motorcycle lovers and those seeking an alternative mode of transportation.
